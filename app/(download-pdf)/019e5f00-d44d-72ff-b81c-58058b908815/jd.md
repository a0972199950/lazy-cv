## About Fun.xyz

Fun.xyz builds crypto payment infrastructure that makes onchain value transfer intuitive, secure, and seamless for everyday users and developers. Its flagship product, **Checkout**, is a high-conversion payment solution that lets users complete any onchain action using assets from self-custodial wallets, centralized exchanges, or on-ramped fiat from cards and bank accounts. By aggregating routing and exposing users' full asset portfolios at the point of sale, Checkout helps dApps drive faster, stickier, more engaged users without stitching together multiple providers or spinning up new chains.

If you believe anyone, anywhere should be able to purchase anything with any asset, Fun.xyz is a place to build that future.

## About the Role

Fun.xyz is hiring Product Engineers (mid, senior, staff) to design and scale the SDKs and product surfaces that power Checkout across web2 and web3 environments. This team owns user-facing flows and developer integrations, balancing performance, reliability, and UX for dApps and financial platforms integrating crypto payments. You will work directly with protocol and backend teams to solve technically complex, product-centric problems for onchain payments at scale.

This is an in-person role in Midtown Manhattan, four days per week (Mon–Thu), with optional WFH Fridays.
