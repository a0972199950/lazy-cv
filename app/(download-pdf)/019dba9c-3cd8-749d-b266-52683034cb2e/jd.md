# Visa — Staff Software Engineer

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/view/4399623602/
**Company:** Visa

## Company Description

Visa is a world leader in payments technology, facilitating transactions between consumers, merchants, financial institutions and government entities across more than 200 countries and territories, dedicated to uplifting everyone, everywhere by being the best way to pay and be paid.

## Job Description

Visa's Technology Organization is a community of problem solvers and innovators reshaping the future of commerce. We operate the world's most sophisticated processing networks capable of handling more than 65k secure transactions a second across 80M merchants, 15k Financial Institutions, and billions of everyday people. While working with us you'll get to work on complex distributed systems and solve massive scale problems centered on new payment flows, business and data solutions, cyber security, and B2C platforms.

### The Opportunity

We are looking for Versatile, curious, and energetic Software Engineers who embrace solving complex challenges on a global scale.

As a Staff Software Engineer, you will be a senior individual contributor in a multi‑functional development team inventing, designing, building, and testing software products that reach a truly global customer base and power Visa's next generation of payment products. You will lead complex technical initiatives while mentoring engineers and influencing engineering standards across teams.

### The Work itself

- Design code and systems that touch 40% of the world population while influencing Visa's internal standards for scalability, security, and reusability.
- Collaborate multi-functionally to create design artifacts and develop best-in-class software solutions.
- Actively contribute to product quality improvements, valuable service technology, and new business flows in diverse agile squads.
- Develop robust and scalable products intended for merchants, B2B, and B2G solutions.
- Leverage innovative technologies to build the next generation of Payment Services, Transaction Platforms, Real-Time Payments, and Buy Now Pay Later Technology.
- Mentorship and continued learning opportunities.
