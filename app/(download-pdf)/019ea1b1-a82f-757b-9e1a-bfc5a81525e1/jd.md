- A degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field

- Experience with building and deploying your solutions, for example in cloud environments like AWS, Azure, GCP
- Strong understanding and appreciation of agile methodologies
- Communication skills that allow you to coordinate between business and technical stakeholders
- Openness and proactiveness allowing you to respond to changes with agility, while learning in the process
- Experience in leading teams and in contributing to the overall strategy and direction of a project is a plus
